We identified students, estate police, shuttle administration (supervisor) and, shuttle drivers as the primary stakeholders. In order to identify the needs of the students, we carried out an observation of students using the shuttle service. This observation was held between September and December of 2015. A short interview was also conducted with the head shuttle driver of the campus where we learnt about the needs of the shuttle administration office. We further investigated the university’s shuttle website to learn more about how the current system works. In addition to which, we conducted an online survey which specifically targeted the students to evoke their views and suggestions
